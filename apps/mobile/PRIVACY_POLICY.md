# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: May 28, 2026**

DailyDue ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application DailyDue (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms, please do not access or use the App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Personal Data
When you register an account or subscribe to our premium services, we may collect certain personally identifiable information, including:
*   **Authentication Data:** Email address and profile information if you register or log in using Google Sign-In or other federated identity providers.
*   **Subscription Details:** Transaction indicators and status tokens related to in-app purchases processed securely via Google Play Billing. We do not collect or store your credit card or financial payment details.

### B. App Data (Local Ledger & Customers)
DailyDue is designed with an offline-first architecture. All transaction histories, customer profiles, debt ledgers, and contact information you record in the App are **stored locally on your device** by default.
*   **Local Storage:** Your client lists, amounts owed, due dates, and transaction logs remain securely stored on your physical device.
*   **Cloud Sync (Optional):** If you create an account, your local ledger data is securely backed up and synchronized to our secure Supabase database to allow multi-device access and prevent data loss.

### C. Device Permissions
To provide features within the App, we may request the following system permissions:
*   **Local Authentication (Biometrics / Fingerprint):** Used strictly on your device to secure your App lock settings (PIN or biometric access). We never collect, transmit, or store your biometric data.
*   **Camera & Photo Library:** Used only when you choose to capture or select a profile picture for a customer in your ledger.

---

## 2. Third-Party Services We Use

We utilize highly secure third-party services to facilitate authentication, data storage, and payment processing:
*   **Supabase:** Used to securely store and synchronize your optional cloud-backed ledger data. All transmissions are fully encrypted via HTTPS.
*   **Google Sign-In:** Used to provide a safe, passwordless login experience.
*   **Google Play Billing:** Used to process premium subscription payments securely under Google's payment policies.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:
*   Provide, operate, and maintain the App's core services.
*   Securely synchronize your optional cloud ledger backup.
*   Improve App performance and optimize user experience.
*   Validate and manage your active premium subscription state.

We **never sell, rent, trade, or share** your personal data or ledger transactions with third-party advertisers or marketers.

---

## 4. Data Security & Retention

### A. Security
We implement industry-standard administrative, technical, and physical security measures to safeguard your information. Offline ledger data is restricted to your device's sandbox. Online sync traffic is encrypted in transit using Transport Layer Security (TLS).

### B. Data Control & Deletion
You have complete control over your data.
*   You can edit or delete any customer record or transaction directly inside the App.
*   If you wish to fully delete your account and purge all synchronized cloud data permanently, you can do so at any time using the account deletion options available in the App Settings.

---

## 5. Children's Privacy
Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will immediately delete this from our servers.
